Our purpose

Our purpose is to develop in men's lives Christ-like attributes and, second, to transform society so that the world may be a better and more peaceful place in which to live.

Our Goals

Our goal is to bring more people into the house of God and share the gospel and order to save us from our eternal sin and teach people about the life of Christ

Our Vision

Our vision is to see people saved, healed, set free, discipled, equipped, empowered, and serving.
